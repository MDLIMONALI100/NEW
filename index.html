About

I’m MD Limon Ali — a web developer who builds with code and writes with heart.

My journey began on an old laptop, where curiosity met a blank screen — and slowly turned into a purpose. From writing lines of HTML to telling stories as a journalist, I’ve always been drawn to things that make people feel, connect, and think.
